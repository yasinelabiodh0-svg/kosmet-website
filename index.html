FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)? +
AEO is the practice of optimizing your business to appear in AI-generated answers. While SEO focuses on ranking in Google search results, AEO ensures you're recommended when people ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Claude for suggestions.
How is this different from traditional SEO? +
Traditional SEO targets search engine results pages. AEO targets AI answer engines. The strategies overlap but AEO requires specific technical implementations — structured data, natural language optimization, and authority signals that AI systems prioritize.
How long until I see results? +
Most clients see initial improvements in AI visibility within 30-60 days. Full optimization and consistent recommendations typically take 90 days.
What AI platforms do you optimize for? +
We optimize for ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Google Gemini, and emerging AI search platforms. We stay ahead of the curve as the landscape evolves.
Do you work with all med spas? +
We're selective. We work with established med spas that have strong clinical reputations and are committed to growth. We limit clients in each geographic area to avoid conflicts.
How do you track AI referrals? +
We use custom tracking: UTM parameters, dedicated phone numbers, and consultation booking questions. You'll see exactly which patients came from AI search in your monthly report.
What if we already have good SEO? +
Great! AEO builds on your existing SEO foundation. We'll audit your current AI visibility and identify gaps. Many med spas with strong SEO are still invisible to ChatGPT — we fix that.
Do you work with med spas outside the US? +
Currently, we focus on the US market where AI search adoption is highest. We may expand to Canada and the UK in 2025.
What's included in the $2,000 setup fee? +

What happens during the free audit? +
We analyze your current AI visibility across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude. You'll see where you stand vs competitors, identify gaps, and get a roadmap to dominate AI search.

Yes. Our 90-day guarantee: if you don't show up in AI answers within 90 days, we work free until you do.
How much time does this require from our team? +
Minimal. We handle 95% of the work. You'll need to provide basic business info and review monthly reports (30 minutes/month).

We monitor AI search platforms daily and adapt our strategies. Your monthly fee includes continuous optimization as the landscape evolves.
